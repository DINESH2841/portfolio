Hitachi Energy

Aug 2025 – Nov 2025

Project: Travel Request Management System

Developed a Travel Request Management System using Next.js (frontend), Express.js (backend), and MSSQL (database) integrated with Microsoft SSO for auto-login via company email ID. Designed role-based workflows where employee details (Name, Mail, Employee ID, Job Position, Managers) were auto-fetched from MSSQL, with options to add unlimited employees per request, save drafts, and submit final requests. Implemented a travel details module with dropdowns (purpose, dates, description) and maintained separate databases for saved and submitted data. Automated email notifications: submission mails sent to managers (To) with employees in CC, including request info and approval links; approval triggered confirmation mails to employees (To) and managers (CC). Built a Manager Dashboard for tracking all requests with statuses (Pending, Approved, Rejected). Gained practical exposure in enterprise full-stack development, database integration, and workflow automation.
